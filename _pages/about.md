## <i class="fas fa-flask" style="color: #1e3a5f;"></i> Research Interests

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(280px, 1fr)); gap: 1.5em; margin: 1.5em 0;">
  
  <div style="background: #fff; border-left: 4px solid #8b2d2d; padding: 1.5em; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1); transition: transform 0.2s;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; color: #8b2d2d;">
      <i class="fas fa-shield-alt" style="margin-right: 0.5em;"></i>Software Security
    </h3>
    <p style="margin-bottom: 0; line-height: 1.6;">
      Software systems are vulnerable to security issues. My research interests include but are not limited to software vulnerability detection, management, and mitigation, as well as privacy protection. Our goal is to identify potential security risks and prevent the leakage of sensitive privacy information (e.g., LLM privacy).
    </p>
  </div>

  <div style="background: #fff; border-left: 4px solid #1e3a5f; padding: 1.5em; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1); transition: transform 0.2s;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; color: #1e3a5f;">
      <i class="fas fa-network-wired" style="margin-right: 0.5em;"></i>Software Supply Chain (AI4SE)
    </h3>
    <p style="margin-bottom: 0; line-height: 1.6;">
      Software systems are inherently complex. Achieving a clear assessment of security, maintenance, and legal risks in the software supply chain, along with developing effective mitigation strategies, remains a significant challenge. Our mission is to simplify this process by providing comprehensive evaluations and actionable solutions.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div style="background: #fff; border-left: 4px solid #2d8b5f; padding: 1.5em; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.1); transition: transform 0.2s;">
    <h3 style="margin-top: 0; color: #2d8b5f;">
      <i class="fas fa-brain" style="margin-right: 0.5em;"></i>AI/LLM Supply Chain (SE4AI)
    </h3>
    <p style="margin-bottom: 0; line-height: 1.6;">
      AI systems (e.g., models, datasets, code, plugins) suffer from similar risks but with different root causes. We aim to evaluate and mitigate risks in AI throughout the lifecycle of software systems, focusing on model supply chain governance, dependency recovery, and risk analysis.
    </p>
  </div>

</div>
